Our Current Projects

Laying, sanding and polishing of wooden staircase

In this project we pinned the sanded and polished wooden boards to the floor and the walls then repolished it with woodoc, the project is nearing completion with a few more steps yet to be layed and repolished.

Wooden door and architrave

Here we removed the door and old door frame that was there, we sanded the door to remove the old woodoc coat that was there, then reapplied all three coats to give it a new look. We bought new architraves and saded them and polished them too then refitted everything back carefully. the project is complete.

Floor Sanding

This project required us to remove the old wooden floor blocks that were originally there and replace then with new wooden floor planks. After pinning them down, we sanded the floor with4 different types of sandpaper, we then applied all three coats of woodoc to give a nice shining look.
