Our Story, mission & values

We believe in:

  • Trust

  • Integrity

  • Merit

Founded in 2003, Fulbright Enterprise has grown from a small local builder to a trusted name in the construction industry. With over 20 years of experience, we specialize in residential, commercial, and custom construction projects.

Our mission is to make sure that your dream home becomes reality. We believe in making sure that your dreams come true,

We help in creating and perfecting your design. We make sure that your design is recreated better than the blueprint. There is no design that is above us!!

We make sure to give accurate timeframes, our good project management skills means that we are able to manage multiple projcets at once despite the distance. Everything is done with transparency!!! We belive in constant communication between us and the client, nothing is done without the clients consent.

Trust and Hardwork are core values that we uphold. We have a hardworking team that outputs excellent results.

Wooden Floors, ceilings, roofs, tiling, doors, wooden window frames, painting and builing and many more, there is nothing we cannot do,

We have travelled and worked with many companies around Zimbabwe and left a good image wherever we went, for example, First pack Zimbabwe were we did floor tiling. Our goal is to continue bringing quality results to our clients. We have also established a name for ourselves in areas such as, Nyanga, Mutorashanga, Glen Forest, Masvingo, Chegutu, Karoi, and many other destinations.
